Astraea, 2021
Regular, 221 glyphs

Astraea is a revival of Astrée by Robert Girard. For this revival, I wanted to be close to the historical inspiration of Garaldes and Elzevir.
My version of Astrée offers a variety of ligatures and special glyphs for stylistic uppercase.
To use Astraea for your project, simply request it via email.

❮ ❯

Lady Spider, 2023
Italic, 70 glyphs

Lady Spider is an italic typeface that draws inspiration from Flemish calligraphy, specifically
the calligraphy of Maria Strick. The font design
is inspired by spiderwebs, with subtle connections between the letters and lines of text, creating
a visual link between the ascenders and descenders of the letters.
Work in Progress

Pendragon, 2022
Regular and Italic, 155 Glyphs

Pendragon is a display font that comes in regular and italic versions. It features a unique set of alternative glyphs and ligatures, inspired by the medieval successors of the Roman capitalis and uncials. The font’s name has also served as an inspiration for several alternate versions, with the ‘g’ character evocative of a dragon’s tail.
To use Pendragon for your project, simply request it via email.
