I’m Céline Jouandet, a French-Brazilian type and graphic designer based in Amsterdam. With a Master’s degree in Typography from ECV Paris and a diploma from Type Paris 2022, I specialise in Type design.
My baroque style draws inspiration from calligraphy, arts, design, cinema, and nature.
feel free to contact me for projects or a coffee together.

  • LET'S WORK TOGETHER

  • If you need a unique logo, typeface, or visual identity for your brand,
    or help for an editorial project:
    Get in touch with me via email to schedule a call and discuss your needs.
    I offer a flexible approach to custom solutions tailored to your specific needs.
    My typical process includes:
  • 1. Cost and time estimate provided
  • 2. Research and exploration phase with initial presentation PDF for feedback.
  • 3. Second PDF for comprehensive view and direction approval
     with second round of feedback.
  • 4. Finalisation of the project with almost-final result
     and final round of feedback and approval.
  • 5. Final presentation sent.
  • 6. Final payment made &Deliverables sent in the requested formats.
